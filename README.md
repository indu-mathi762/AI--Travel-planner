# Travel-planner
An AI-powered travel planning assistant that generates personalized destination suggestions, day-wise itineraries, budget estimates, and packing checklists based on user preferences.
🌍 AI Travel Planner ✈️

«Your Smart AI Companion for Stress-Free Travel Planning»

📌 Overview

Planning a trip should be exciting—not overwhelming.

AI Travel Planner is an intelligent travel assistant that creates personalized travel experiences based on your interests, budget, travel duration, and preferences. Whether you're planning a solo adventure, a family vacation, or a business trip, this AI helps you organize everything in just a few clicks.

---

✨ Features

🗺️ Personalized Destination Recommendations

- Suggests destinations based on your interests.
- Supports nature, adventure, beaches, culture, food, shopping, and more.

📅 Day-Wise Travel Itinerary

- Generates a detailed schedule for each day.
- Includes sightseeing, meals, transportation, and leisure activities.

💰 Smart Budget Estimation

- Calculates estimated expenses for:
  - Accommodation
  - Food
  - Transportation
  - Attractions
  - Shopping
  - Miscellaneous expenses

🎒 Intelligent Packing Checklist

- Recommends items based on:
  - Destination
  - Weather
  - Trip duration
  - Travel type

🌦️ Weather-Aware Planning

- Suggests clothing and essentials according to expected weather conditions.

🤖 AI-Powered Recommendations

- Delivers customized travel suggestions using user preferences and intelligent decision-making.

🚀 How It Works

1. Enter your travel preferences.
2. Choose your budget.
3. Select trip duration.
4. Mention your interests.
5. Let AI generate your personalized travel plan.
6. Explore your itinerary, budget estimate, and packing checklist.
